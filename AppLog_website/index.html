Project Description

This application is part of a research study being conducted at Qatar University entitled "Securing Information Flow of Android Apps without Firmware Modification".


This application will compile a list of other Apps installed on your phone and send this information to us in order to help us determine information about Apps used by residents of Qatar. We will log the following information and retain the data for further analysis later:

  • Date and time of the request
  • IP address of the request
  • Your advertising ID
  • Version of Android you are running
  • Details of the applications installed on your device

The application will allow you to see exactly what information is collected prior to sending it.

Your privacy and confidentiality is important to us. While the IP address could potentially be used to uniquely identify you, our usage will be limited to identifying your country and to ensure that users do not abuse our system. Any publications based on this data will only use information in aggregate form, information about individual users will not be published or identified.
